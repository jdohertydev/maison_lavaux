About Us

Antoine Lambert - Founder of Maison Lavaux

Meet Antoine Lambert

Antoine Lambert, the visionary behind Maison Lavaux, has dedicated his life to crafting exceptional scents that inspire and captivate. With a passion rooted in tradition and a commitment to excellence, Antoine has transformed Maison Lavaux into a symbol of sophistication and artistry in the world of perfumes.

Our Story

Antoine Lambert grew up in the picturesque region of Lavaux, surrounded by lush vineyards and the intoxicating scents of blooming flowers. From a young age, he felt drawn to the world of fragrances, spending countless hours blending natural essences from his family garden.

However, his journey was not without challenges. Leaving his small village, Antoine traveled to Paris to learn the craft of perfumery. There, he faced stiff competition and initial failures, but he remained undeterred. Through perseverance, he honed his craft and began creating scents that resonated deeply with emotions and memories.

Maison Lavaux was born from this vision. Today, Antoine Lambert and his team continue to create perfumes that tell stories, bringing the essence of Lavaux to customers worldwide.
