Your privacy matters to us at Maison Lavaux

Welcome to Maison Lavaux. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website and services. As a Paris-based perfume shop, we are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and ensuring the privacy of our customers.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Order Information: Billing and shipping details, payment information, and order history.
  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, and activity on our website (via cookies and analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Send updates on your orders and promotional offers (if consented).
  • Improve our website functionality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping companies, and IT support services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights and customers.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in France.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your shopping experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us analyze website traffic and personalize your experience. By continuing to browse our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You can manage your preferences in your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
